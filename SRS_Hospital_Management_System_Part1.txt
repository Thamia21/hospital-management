1. OVERVIEW
================================================================================

1.1 PURPOSE
-----------
This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) document provides a comprehensive 
description of the Hospital Management System (HMS). The system is designed to 
streamline hospital operations, improve patient care, and enhance administrative 
efficiency through digital automation of core hospital processes.

The HMS will serve as a centralized platform for:
- Patient registration and management
- Appointment scheduling and management
- Medical records and prescription management
- Staff management and leave tracking
- Billing and payment processing
- Real-time communication and notifications
- Multi-language support for South African healthcare facilities

This document is intended for stakeholders, developers, testers, and end-users 
to understand the system's functionality, constraints, and requirements.

1.2 DOCUMENT CONVENTIONS
------------------------
This SRS follows IEEE Std 830-1998 guidelines for software requirements 
specifications. The following conventions are used throughout this document:

- Requirements are numbered hierarchically (e.g., 2.3.1, 2.3.2)
- Priority levels: High, Medium, Low
- Functional requirements are prefixed with "FR-"
- Non-functional requirements are prefixed with "NFR-"
- Use cases are prefixed with "UC-"
- Bold text indicates key terms and concepts
- Italics indicate system responses or user interface elements

1.4 PRODUCT SCOPE
-----------------
The Hospital Management System is a comprehensive web-based application designed 
to digitize and streamline hospital operations in South African healthcare facilities.

**Primary Objectives:**
- Reduce administrative overhead by 40%
- Improve patient care coordination
- Ensure regulatory compliance with South African healthcare standards
- Provide real-time access to patient information
- Support multiple official South African languages
- Enable efficient resource allocation and staff management

**Key Benefits:**
- Enhanced patient safety through digital medical records
- Improved operational efficiency through automated workflows
- Better financial management with integrated billing
- Comprehensive reporting and analytics
- Reduced paperwork and manual errors
- 24/7 system availability for critical healthcare operations

**Scope Boundaries:**
IN SCOPE:
- Patient registration and management
- Appointment scheduling and management
- Medical records and prescription management
- Staff management and leave tracking
- Billing and payment processing
- Multi-language support (11 South African official languages)
- Real-time notifications and communication
- Reporting and analytics dashboard
- User authentication and authorization
- Data backup and recovery

OUT OF SCOPE:
- Medical device integration (Phase 2)
- Laboratory information system integration (Phase 2)
- Pharmacy inventory management (Phase 2)
- Insurance claim processing (Phase 2)
- Telemedicine capabilities (Future enhancement)
- Mobile application (Phase 2)

2. OVERALL DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

2.1 PRODUCT PERSPECTIVE
-----------------------
The Hospital Management System is a new, self-contained web-based application 
designed specifically for South African healthcare facilities. The system operates 
as a comprehensive digital platform that replaces traditional paper-based hospital 
management processes.

**System Context:**
The HMS operates within the broader healthcare ecosystem, interfacing with:
- Hospital staff (doctors, nurses, administrators)
- Patients and their families
- Hospital management and decision-makers
- Regulatory compliance requirements
- Financial and billing systems

**System Architecture:**
- **Frontend**: React.js web application with Material-UI components
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express.js framework
- **Database**: MongoDB for data persistence
- **Authentication**: JWT-based secure authentication
- **Communication**: Email notifications via SMTP
- **Deployment**: Cloud-based hosting with scalability support

**Key System Interfaces:**
- Web browser interface for all user interactions
- Email system for automated notifications
- Database management for data persistence
- File system for document storage and retrieval

2.2 CHANGE REQUESTS FOR OLD VOR
-------------------------------
This is a new system development, replacing existing manual and paper-based 
processes. The following legacy system limitations are addressed:

**Previous System Limitations:**
- Manual paper-based patient records leading to data loss and inefficiency
- Phone-based appointment scheduling causing scheduling conflicts
- Manual staff leave tracking resulting in coverage gaps
- Paper-based billing prone to errors and delays
- No centralized patient information access
- Language barriers for non-English speaking patients
- Limited reporting and analytics capabilities

**Improvements in New System:**
- Digital patient records with backup and recovery
- Automated appointment scheduling with conflict detection
- Electronic leave management with approval workflows
- Integrated billing with payment tracking
- Centralized database with role-based access
- Multi-language support for all 11 South African official languages
- Comprehensive reporting and analytics dashboard
- Real-time notifications and communication

2.3 PRODUCT FUNCTIONS
---------------------
The Hospital Management System provides the following major functions:

**FR-001: User Management and Authentication**
- User registration with role-based access (Patient, Doctor, Nurse, Admin)
- Secure login with email verification
- Password management and recovery
- User profile management
- Session management and security

**FR-002: Patient Management**
- Patient registration with comprehensive personal information
- Patient search and retrieval
- Medical history tracking
- Emergency contact management
- Patient dashboard with health summary

**FR-003: Appointment Management**
- Appointment scheduling with doctor/nurse availability
- Appointment modification and cancellation
- Automated conflict detection
- Appointment reminders via email
- Calendar integration and time slot management

**FR-004: Medical Records Management**
- Digital medical record creation and maintenance
- Prescription management and tracking
- Medical history documentation
- Secure access to patient records
- Medical document upload and storage

**FR-005: Staff Management**
- Staff profile management
- Leave request submission and approval
- Staff scheduling and availability tracking
- Department and specialization management
- Staff performance tracking

**FR-006: Billing and Payment Management**
- Invoice generation and management
- Payment processing and tracking
- Outstanding balance monitoring
- Payment history and receipts
- Financial reporting

**FR-007: Communication and Notifications**
- Email notifications for appointments, prescriptions, and billing
- Real-time system notifications
- Multi-language communication support
- Emergency contact notifications

**FR-008: Reporting and Analytics**
- Patient statistics and demographics
- Appointment analytics and trends
- Staff performance reports
- Financial reports and billing analytics
- System usage and performance metrics

**FR-009: Multi-Language Support**
- Interface translation for 11 South African official languages
- Language preference management
- Cultural-appropriate content adaptation
- Accessibility compliance

**FR-010: System Administration**
- User role and permission management
- System configuration and settings
- Data backup and recovery management
- Security monitoring and audit trails
- System maintenance and updates

2.4 USER CLASSES AND CHARACTERISTICS
------------------------------------

**UC-001: Hospital Administrators**
- **Primary Role**: System oversight and management
- **Technical Expertise**: Medium to High
- **Responsibilities**:
  - User account management and role assignment
  - System configuration and settings management
  - Report generation and analysis
  - Staff management and leave approval
  - Financial oversight and billing management
- **Frequency of Use**: Daily
- **Key Features**: Admin dashboard, user management, reporting, system settings

- **Primary Role**: Patient care and medical decision-making
- **Technical Expertise**: Medium
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Patient consultation and treatment
  - Medical record documentation
  - Prescription management
  - Appointment scheduling and management
  - Leave request submission
- **Frequency of Use**: Daily
- **Key Features**: Patient records, appointment management, prescription tools, medical documentation

**UC-003: Nurses**
- **Primary Role**: Patient care support and medical assistance
- **Technical Expertise**: Medium
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Patient care coordination
  - Medical record updates
  - Appointment assistance
  - Medication administration tracking
  - Leave request submission
- **Frequency of Use**: Daily
- **Key Features**: Patient records, appointment management, medication tracking, care coordination

- **Primary Role**: Healthcare service recipients
- **Technical Expertise**: Low to Medium
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Personal information management
  - Appointment booking and management
  - Medical history review
  - Bill payment and financial management
  - Communication with healthcare providers
- **Frequency of Use**: As needed (weekly to monthly)
- **Key Features**: Patient portal, appointment booking, medical history, billing, notifications

**UC-005: Support Staff (Receptionists/Administrative)**
- **Primary Role**: Administrative support and patient coordination
- **Technical Expertise**: Medium
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Patient registration and check-in
  - Appointment scheduling assistance
  - Billing and payment processing
  - General system support
  - Information management
- **Frequency of Use**: Daily
- **Key Features**: Patient registration, appointment management, billing, basic reporting
